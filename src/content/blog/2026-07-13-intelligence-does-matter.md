Please do not say dumb things like "intelligence does not matter." That's an admission of you being dumb.

For most of us, what you do from 9 to 5 does not matter. Yes, it feeds your family. That's it. You are not smart enough to move humanity forward. All you do is write emails with ChatGPT and update Word and Excel spreadsheets. Yes, what you do does not require intelligence.

Saying "intelligence does not matter" is the same as saying "you will never use math." Tell that to AI researchers or rocket scientists. Telling someone "you will never use math" is like calling that person stupid.

I know you've never been exposed to an environment that requires a huge amount of brainpower. But don't let that fool you into believing intelligence does not matter, and that it's fake kindness or fake leadership that's more important. Those attributes matter for your own good. But they don't have the type of impact great intelligence brings to humanity.

Kindness didn't cure all these diseases. Kindness didn't build airplanes. Kindness didn't build CPUs or GPUs. Everything that makes your life comfortable and extends your life came from people with high intelligence.

I know you cannot grasp the other worlds that exist outside your tiny bubble where intelligence and math do not matter. But they do.

So please stop saying dumb things. That makes you look dumb.
